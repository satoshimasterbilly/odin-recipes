Apple-Cake Recipe

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 213 calories; protein 2.6g; carbohydrates 33.8g; fat 8.3g; cholesterol 15.5mg; sodium 208.9mg. Full Nutrition


Ingredients
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cups peeled and chopped apple
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/2 cup raisins
Steps 
  1. Step 1
    Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a fluted tube pan (such as BundtÂ®)
  2. Step 2
    Sift flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt together in a bowl. Set aside
  3. Step 3
    Mix sugar, egg, and oil together in another bowl. Stir in the flour mixture just until combined; fold in apple, walnuts, and raisins
  4. Step 4
    Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the cake comes out clean, about 1 hour

Cook's Note:


You can use dates instead of raisins, and any kind of chopped nut that you prefer
